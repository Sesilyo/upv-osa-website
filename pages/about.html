About Us

In support of the academic programs of the University, the Office of Student Affairs or OSA, headed by the Director, is committed to the holistic development of students. It has main office located at the College Union Building (CUB) in the Miag-ao campus, and an extension office in the Iloilo City campus. It delivers an integrated program of services to meet the students’ multifarious needs throughout the two campuses (Miag-ao and Iloilo City).

Major Services Offered:

  • Guidance & Counseling
  • Student Organizatiions & Counseling
  • Scholarships & Financial Assistance
  • Residential Services

Vision

In support of UPV's vision, OSA strives to offer empowering experiences that provide meaning and vitality to students' journey as they prepareto make their marks in diverse, dynamic, and global communities.

Mission

OSA is committed to continuously provide innovative programs and services that foster holistic development of students.

OSA transcends boundaries to ensure that students get the best experience possible.

OSA creates spaces to promote diversity, equity, and inclusitivity as well as ensure the safety and well-being of students.
